AUTUMN/ WINTER 2019

Next year symbolises an important milestone for Rotary Watches as it approaches its 125 year birthday. Ahead of this momentous occasion, Rotary has delved into its past to bring together a celebration of heritage and iconic design. Spanning across delicate timepieces that sparkle and shine, to rugged models that deliver a stylish way for you to become your best self, this season’s collection is a testament to the worldwide reputation of Rotary Watches for its watch making excellence and its determination to always deliver unparalleled quality timepieces at an exceptional price. It is no wonder, that they were recently awarded Watch Brand of the Year at the Retail Jeweller UK Jewellery Awards 2019.

GREENWICH

The complex artistry of the skeletal Greenwich family, with stunning exposed automatic movement, has quickly become an iconic staple for Rotary Watches. This season Rotary’s Skeleton offering has evolved, maintaining the core DNA of Greenwich, but incorporating a stunning shaped ‘cage’ structure which creates additional space and light surrounding the self-winding movement. Each ‘strut’ feature within acts as an index and is topped with a luminescent hour marker: ensuring this timepiece is the ultimate blend of functionality and style. Each timepiece is limited to 300 pieces.

HERITAGE

Introducing a new watch family of limited edition pieces: Heritage. Three watches with effortless style and timeless character have been carefully selected from Rotary’s archive to inspire the new family.
